Shipping and Delivery Policy

Effective Date: 02/11/2023

At Ellandakunta Milk Cooperative Dairy (EMCD), we strive to deliver high-quality dairy products and services to our valued customers in a timely and efficient manner. This Shipping and Delivery Policy outlines the process for the shipping and delivery of products purchased from our website. By placing an order with EMCD, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Shipping Areas

We currently offer shipping to locations within Ellandakunta, Jammikunta, and other selected regions. Please note that some products may have restricted shipping areas due to their perishable nature or other factors.

If you are located outside of our serviceable areas, please contact us directly to inquire about potential shipping options.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees are calculated based on the delivery location, the size of the order, and the nature of the products being delivered. The applicable shipping charge will be displayed at checkout before you finalize your order.

  • Free Shipping: EMCD may offer free shipping promotions or discounts on shipping for certain orders, which will be clearly communicated on the website or at checkout.
  • Flat Rate Shipping: In certain cases, a flat shipping rate may apply depending on the products ordered and the delivery location.

3. Order Processing Time

Orders placed on the EMCD website are typically processed within 8 hours of receipt. However, please note that processing times may vary depending on product availability, holidays, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day. Processing times do not include shipping time.

4. Delivery Time

Delivery times vary based on the shipping location, the products ordered, and the chosen shipping method. Once your order is processed and shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with tracking details (if applicable) to track your order.

  • Estimated Delivery Time: Typically, delivery within Ellandakunta, Jammikunta is expected to take 8 hours.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and can vary depending on factors such as weather, transportation delays, or other events outside our control.

5. Shipping Methods

We partner with trusted delivery carriers to ensure your products reach you in the best condition. The shipping method may vary based on the size and nature of the order.

  • Standard Shipping: This is the default shipping option, and delivery times will depend on your location.
  • Expedited Shipping: For faster delivery, expedited shipping options may be available at checkout (subject to availability).

6. Delivery of Perishable Products

As EMCD deals with fresh dairy products, we ensure that all perishable items are carefully packaged and shipped under controlled conditions to maintain their freshness. However, please keep in mind the following:

  • Perishable items such as milk, yogurt, and other dairy products may have a shorter shelf life. Therefore, it is important to ensure that someone is available to receive the package promptly upon delivery.
  • We recommend storing perishable items in a refrigerator immediately upon receipt to maintain their quality and safety.

7. Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email containing your tracking number and details about the carrier used for delivery. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your delivery.

If you have any questions about your order or need assistance with tracking, please contact our customer support team.

8. Failed Deliveries and Return to Sender

If the delivery attempt fails due to incorrect address information or if the package is not collected in a timely manner, the carrier may return the package to EMCD. In such cases:

  • Address Verification: If the shipping address provided was incorrect, the customer is responsible for providing the correct address and covering any additional shipping charges.
  • Return to Sender: If a package is returned to EMCD due to failed delivery attempts, we will notify you. Reshipping charges will apply if you wish to have the product resent.

9. Damaged or Missing Items

We take great care in packaging your order to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition. However, in the rare event that your order arrives damaged or items are missing, please contact us within 1 days of receiving the delivery with the following details:

  • Order number
  • Description and photos of damaged or missing items
  • Proof of delivery

Once we receive this information, we will assess the situation and, if necessary, offer a replacement or refund.

10. Shipping Restrictions

There may be certain restrictions on shipping certain items due to their nature, such as perishable dairy products, hazardous materials, or items with limited shelf life. EMCD reserves the right to refuse to ship to certain regions or cancel an order if the product cannot be shipped safely.

11. Customer Responsibilities

As the customer, you are responsible for ensuring that the shipping address provided is correct and complete. EMCD is not responsible for any delays or issues caused by incorrect or incomplete address information.

Additionally, you are responsible for accepting the delivery of your order when it arrives. If you are unavailable to receive the delivery, please ensure someone is there to accept it on your behalf.
